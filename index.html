Who We Are

Engineering with
Purpose

Founded in 1998, Apex Engineering Group has grown from a regional consultancy into one of the world's most trusted engineering firms. We blend deep technical expertise with innovative thinking to deliver structures that stand the test of time.

Our multidisciplinary team of over 800 engineers, architects, and specialists operates from 12 offices worldwide. Every project we undertake reflects our core belief: that great engineering transforms communities, economies, and lives.

We have completed over 340 landmark projects across sectors including transportation, energy, commercial, and residential infrastructure — consistently on time and within budget.
